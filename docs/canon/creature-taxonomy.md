# Creature Taxonomy

> Official classification of backyard fauna as recognized by Sakura's operational doctrine.
> Threat tiers are assigned based on observed Sakura response intensity.

---

## Threat Classification System

| Tier | Label | Sakura Response |
|---|---|---|
| S | Extreme Threat | Full sprint, bark sequence, tunnel vision |
| A | High Threat | Immediate engagement, sustained pursuit |
| B | Moderate Threat | Active interest, controlled approach |
| C | Low Threat | Acknowledgment, optional investigation |
| ? | Unknown | Classified pending vorg confirmation |

---

## Confirmed Species

### Squirrels — Tier S
**Status:** Primary adversary  
**Population:** Persistent. Apparently inexhaustible.  
**Tactics:** Use fence line as escape route. Appear to taunt. Possibly organized.  
**Notes:** Years of engagement. Zero confirmed catches by Sakura. Squirrels appear unconcerned. This is escalating.

---

### Rabbits — Tier A
**Status:** All presumed suspicious  
**Population:** Garden sector primarily. Some open field incursions.  
**Tactics:** Stillness as camouflage. Sudden explosive movement. Use of garden beds as concealment.  
**Notes:** Sakura takes rabbits seriously in a way that suggests prior incidents. Nature of those incidents: undisclosed.

---

### Birds — Tier A
**Status:** High priority. Aerial engagement history.  
**Population:** Variable. Seasonal. Migratory unknowns a concern.  
**Tactics:** Flight. Primarily flight.  
**Notes:** One confirmed catch. Sakura does not discuss it. The family does.

---

### Bugs — Tier C
**Status:** Casual targets  
**Population:** High. Especially in summer.  
**Tactics:** Low-altitude evasion. Some species have chemical deterrents (Sakura has not learned this).  
**Notes:** Sakura will chase a bug but it is not her life's work. Yet.

---

### Vorgs — Tier ?
**Status:** Existence unconfirmed  
**Population:** Unknown  
**Tactics:** Unknown. Possibly temporal?  
**Notes:** See `vorg-mythology.md` for full documentation. Sakura appears to sense something in The Unknown Regions. This is the primary evidence for their existence.

## Classification Notes

- This taxonomy reflects Sakura's perspective, not a naturalist's.
- Threat tier does not indicate actual danger to Sakura.
- New species must be classified before they appear in game events.
- Vorgs may require their own tier system.
