# Backyard Map — Named Zones

> The backyard is not just a yard. It is a hunting ground with distinct sectors, each with its own creature population density, terrain features, and strategic significance.

## Zone Profiles

### The Patio Perimeter
**Distance from house:** Adjacent  
**Terrain:** Hardscape, patio furniture, some potted plants  
**Creature activity:** Low-medium. Occasional bird landings. Bugs.  
**Strategic value:** Staging area. Sakura begins most hunts here.  
**Notes:** Excellent sightlines to the Open Field. Sakura monitors from here before committing to pursuit.

---

### The Open Field
**Distance from house:** Mid-range  
**Terrain:** Grass, open, no cover for prey  
**Creature activity:** Medium. Squirrels crossing between fence line and trees. Bird flyovers.  
**Strategic value:** Primary pursuit zone. Speed advantage to Sakura.  
**Notes:** Most chases that end in "almost caught" occur here.

---

### The Garden Sector
**Distance from house:** Mid-range, left side  
**Terrain:** Raised beds, mulch, some low cover  
**Creature activity:** High. Rabbits. Always rabbits.  
**Strategic value:** Contested. Rabbits know about the garden. Sakura knows about the rabbits.  
**Notes:** The garden smells interesting. This complicates tactical focus.

---

### The Fence Line
**Distance from house:** Far boundary, east side  
**Terrain:** Grass, fence posts, shrub coverage on exterior  
**Creature activity:** Very high. Primary squirrel infiltration and escape route.  
**Strategic value:** Critical. Controlling the fence line means controlling squirrel access.  
**Notes:** Squirrels use the fence as a highway. Sakura patrols it regularly. The patrol appears to have no deterrent effect on the squirrels.

---

### The Unknown Regions
**Distance from house:** Far corner, maximum range  
**Terrain:** Unknown. Overgrown? Possibly.  
**Creature activity:** Unconfirmed. Reported movement. Possible vorg territory.  
**Strategic value:** Unknown and therefore concerning.  
**Notes:** Sakura monitors this zone but has not fully committed to exploration. This is wise or ominous depending on what is back there.

---

## Map Notes

- This map is approximate and based on observed Sakura behavior patterns.
- Zone boundaries are behavioral, not physical.
- The map should be updated as new canonical incidents provide additional data.
- Vorgs, if real, may not be confined to named zones.
