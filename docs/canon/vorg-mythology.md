# Vorg Mythology

> What is a vorg? This is the correct question.

---

## Current Status

**Existence:** Unconfirmed  
**Evidence:** Circumstantial but suggestive  
**Sakura's position:** She knows something. She is not saying what.  
**Family consensus:** Divided

---

## Known Facts (Confirmed)

- The word "vorg" exists in family vocabulary.
- It refers to something. Possibly several somethings.
- Sakura demonstrates anomalous behavior near The Unknown Regions that is consistent with vorg detection.
- Vorgs, if real, are not squirrels. They are not birds. They are not rabbits. They are something else.

---

## Reported Characteristics

*All reports unconfirmed. Logged for completeness.*

- May be nocturnal, or at least crepuscular
- Associated with The Unknown Regions and the far fence corner
- Not observed directly, only inferred from Sakura's alert pattern
- Possibly leave traces (unspecified)
- Sakura has not yet committed to full engagement — this is notable

## Implications for Gameplay

- Vorgs should not be confirmed until the family confirms them canonically.
- The vorg threat level in-game should remain at "?" until then.
- Vorgs, once confirmed, will almost certainly require their own game mode.
- The Unknown Regions cannot be fully mapped until vorg status is resolved.
