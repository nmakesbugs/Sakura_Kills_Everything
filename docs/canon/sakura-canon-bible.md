# Sakura Canon Bible

> This document is Tier 1 source of truth. Nothing in this game may contradict it.

---

## Identity

**Full name:** Sakura  
**Species:** Dog  
**Classification:** Apex backyard predator  
**Jurisdiction:** The Backyard and all adjacent territories  
**Status:** Active. Extremely active.

---

## Confirmed Behaviors

- Sakura hunts with complete sincerity. She does not know she is funny.
- Sakura has never successfully caught a squirrel. This does not discourage her.
- Sakura has caught at least one bird. This is mentioned with pride.
- Sakura patrols the fence line on a self-determined schedule.
- Sakura monitors the yard from interior windows when denied outdoor access.
- Sakura responds to squirrel appearances with immediate and total commitment.
- Sakura has a signature alert bark. Family members can identify the specific target by bark pattern.
- Sakura is not afraid of larger animals. This is considered unwise by outside observers.

- Determined beyond what is medically advisable.
- Joyful. The hunt is not a burden. It is a calling.
- Focused under distraction except when treats are involved.
- Does not hold grudges (against family members). Squirrels: unknown.
