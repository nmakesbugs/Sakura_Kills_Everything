# Photo Requirements

> Required photography of Sakura for game asset production.
> All photos must be of the actual Sakura. No substitutions.

---

## Required Poses

### Idle — Standing
**Use:** Home screen, map view, downtime between hunts  
**Shot:** Full body, side profile or slight three-quarter  
**Expression:** Alert, ears up, scanning  
**Background:** Solid or easily removable

---

### Idle — Sitting
**Use:** Menu screens, loading states, Sakura Canon  
**Shot:** Full body, facing camera or slight angle  
**Expression:** Patient, watchful  
**Background:** Solid or easily removable

---

### Alert / Spotted
**Use:** Creature detection moment, "vorg alert" state  
**Shot:** Body forward-leaning, ears forward, weight shifted  
**Expression:** Full attention locked on target  
**Background:** Removable

---

### Pounce / Launch
**Use:** Engagement animation start, Duck Hunt  
**Shot:** Motion blur acceptable; captures forward thrust  
**Expression:** Committed. Maximum commitment.  
**Background:** Removable; motion blur on body acceptable

---

### Victory
**Use:** Successful engagement, zone secured, mode complete  
**Shot:** Head up, possibly panting, satisfied posture  
**Expression:** Self-satisfied. She did her job.  
**Background:** Removable

---

### Confused
**Use:** Vorg events, anomaly outcomes, "where did it go?"  
**Shot:** Head tilt, ears slightly askew  
**Expression:** Tilted head, searching  
**Background:** Removable

---

## Photography Notes

- Natural light outdoors preferred — this is a backyard game
- Backyard backgrounds ideal but removable backgrounds work fine
- Multiple shots per pose; pick the most expressive
- 1200px minimum width; 2400px preferred for 2x sprites
- RAW if available; JPEG fine
- Do not stage poses that Sakura would not naturally do

---

## Shot Priority

1. Idle Standing (needed for home screen)
2. Alert / Spotted (needed for Duck Hunt Build 2)
3. Victory (needed for any completion screen)
4. Pounce (needed for engagement animation)
5. Sitting Idle (nice to have)
6. Confused (needed for vorg events — low priority until vorg mode)
