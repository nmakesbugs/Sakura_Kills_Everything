# v0.8E — Episodic Direction + Episode 2 (QA Note)

A note, not a thesis.

## Why "v0.8E"
"E" = episodes. This is the first build that *commits* to the authored-episode direction the
Tanisha Cut proved out (v0.7R). It is not a mature full game — it is the second real episode
plus the reorg that makes episodes the product. `package.json` is bumped to `0.8.0`; the HUD
label is **v0.8E**.

## What changed
- **New product face.** `episodes/index.html` (the *Backyard Incident Files*) lists episodes,
  not modes. Root `index.html` now routes to it.
- **Bird Incident migrated** from `tanisha-cut/` → `episodes/bird-incident/`, refactored onto
  the shared shell. **Story preserved verbatim** — the successful playtest is intact.
- **Episode 2 built:** `episodes/squirrel-surveillance/` — 11 beats, 3 choices, distinct
  reports, squirrel always **Uncaught**.
- **Shared shell extracted** (`episodes/shared/episode-shell.{css,js}`): a small helper, not an
  engine. Each `episode.js` still reads top-to-bottom as a story.
- **Old mode platform demoted** to `legacy/index.html` (reference only). `src/*` untouched and
  still tested.

## What's reused vs. not
- **Reused:** the real Sakura photo (static, framed), the Official-vs-Likely device, the comic
  visual language, the Tanisha-as-witness role, the screenshot-worthy report, Playwright.
- **Not reused (by design):** the incident engine, adventure engine, storage, Canon Archive,
  data files, the mode selector. Episodes do not depend on any of it.

## Canon held
- Squirrels are **never caught.** Every Squirrel Surveillance path ends with squirrel status =
  a variant of *Uncaught* (asserted by a dedicated test). Sakura is sincere and lovable; the
  squirrel is smug and untouchable; failure is funny, not punishing.
- Birds can be caught (Bird Incident unchanged).

## What it tests
72 Playwright specs pass: 51 baseline (now incl. the legacy home) + 21 new (episode index +
root routing; Bird Incident full path & 3 distinct reports; Squirrel Surveillance full path, 3
distinct reports, **every path uncaught**, Play Again, no console errors).

## What Tanisha should react to
- A second incident to try, with a *smug squirrel* she'll recognize.
- The surveillance vignette ("REC ●"), the eye-contact glyph, the dust-puff fence charge.
- The report stamps (Surveillance Successful / Suspect Fled Jurisdiction / Undercover Operation
  Compromised) and the **Squirrel Status: Uncaught** line as the running joke.
- Her own witness notes ("Tanisha is not sure who was surveilling whom.").

## How to play
Open `index.html` (or `episodes/index.html`) directly — no server, no build. ~3–5 min first
play per episode, ~1–2 min replays. Mobile portrait (412×760).

## If it still feels flat
The framework is proven; lean on writing and a little real Sakura action art. If a third
episode (Rabbit at the Garden Frontier) lands as easily as this one did, the pattern is real.
