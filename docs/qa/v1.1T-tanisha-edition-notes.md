# v1.1T — Tanisha Edition (QA Note)

Short, by design. This is a **polish / closing / cohesion** milestone, not a new gameplay one.

## 1. Why v1.1T exists
Four episodes existed, each with its own toy, but they read like four separate demos. v1.1T turns
the prototype into a warmer **Tanisha Edition**: a coherent little collection that ends on a note
that makes clear *who it's for*. No Episode 5, no new mode, no revived platform.

## 2. What was polished
- **Shared delight (all four episodes at once):** subtle beat transitions on render (`beat-fade`
  / `beat-rise`), richer feather/debris bursts (6 → 9 particles, varied size/spin/duration), and
  a slightly snappier button press.
- **Bird:** an extra Tanisha witness line per branch.
- **Squirrel:** an extra Tanisha line per branch (the squirrel exits with unreasonable confidence).
- **Rabbit:** a leaf flourish on the complication and a payoff burst at the lunge (dust on a
  catch, leaves on an escape).
- **Vorg:** an animated concern meter (segments fill in) + a clue-card pop on examine.

## 3. What the closing adds
`episodes/for-tanisha/` — a standalone **“For Tanisha”** screen: glowing Sakura portrait, a short
warm-but-funny message in Sakura's voice, and an official **Dedication report** (Official: "Tanisha's
witness record has strengthened the historical archive beyond measure." / Likely Reality: "She
loves Sakura, and that is why this exists." / Status · Sakura: "Remains unaware. Assumes the medal
was always hers." / case `SKE-0000-T · Thank you, witness.`). Always available (no persistence
gate) as a gold "♥ For Tanisha" card on the index and root.

## 4. How the four episodes tie together
- The index now opens with framing — *"Four backyard incidents. One unreliable dog. One very
  patient witness."* — plus a softer note that these are official records of questionable accuracy.
- Consistent episode numbering, status chips, and end buttons; every episode ends with **Back to
  Incident Files**, and the index ends on the **For Tanisha** card. It reads as a gift, not a menu.

## 5. What Tanisha should react to
- Being named the **Witness of Record** and the closing dedication (the payoff of the whole thing).
- Her extra witness lines across the four episodes (she's the reality anchor in each).
- The small new juice — bursts, the filling concern meter, the leaf flourish.

## 6. What remains for post-production
- Real Sakura *action* art (still framed emoji tokens; the photo stays static per policy).
- Optional completion-gated closing (intentionally skipped — no persistence added).
- A title/launch screen and audio, if this ever ships beyond family.

## Versioning
HUD label **v1.1T** ("T" = Tanisha Edition); `package.json` → `1.1.0`. Polish milestone, not a new
gameplay tier.

## Efficiency
No shell rewrite, no new engine, no storage. Closing is standalone HTML/CSS reusing shell classes.
Delight came mostly from shared additions (so all four benefit) plus a few targeted lines/effects.
Legacy platform, `src/*`, and the episodes' core flows untouched.
